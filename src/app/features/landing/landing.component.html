Best Video and Audio Formats for the Web

Video:

  • MP4 (H.264 + AAC): High compatibility, good quality, and compression.
  • WebM (VP8/VP9 + Opus/Vorbis): Patent-free, good quality, but less compatible with Safari.
  • OGG (Theora + Vorbis): Patent-free, compatible with Firefox, but less used.
  • AVI: High quality, but large files affect load time.

Audio:

  • MP3: Highly compatible, good quality, and compression.
  • AAC: Better quality than MP3 at similar bit rates.
  • OGG (Vorbis): Patent-free, good quality, but less compatible.
  • Opus: Excellent quality, ideal for voice and real-time streaming.

Summary:

  • Video: MP4 is the most compatible, WebM is great for modern compression.
  • Audio: MP3 is universal, AAC is better for quality, Opus is best for streaming.
